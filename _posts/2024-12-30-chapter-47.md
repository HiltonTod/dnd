At the end of the fifty-foot long hallway, a wooden door is closed, with a heavy bar laden across
its front, preventing it from being opened. Dolor looks back to the others for approval
before lifting the metal beam from its place, securing the door—and whatever they'll find beyond it.










Gustaf hurriedly updates the map in his leatherbound journal.
